Business model

Free to use.
Built to earn as Arc grows.

We're early and pre-revenue by choice — first we become the default hub. The core stays free; revenue scales with the ecosystem, across three honest lines:
  • Arc Trials — a small take rate (USDC) on the tester campaigns builders choose to fund.
  • Premium builder tools — analytics, alerts, and richer project pages, on subscription.
  • Data & API — verified, oracle-free Arc metrics licensed to wallets and apps; our exact on-chain data is the differentiator.
We monetize value, never access — and the trust layer is the moat that makes the hub worth building on, not a fee.
