# Campaigns

**Campaigns** (also called trials) are how founders bootstrap early usage of a project on Arc — the growth surface of ArcLens.

## How they work

- A founder creates a campaign with a goal, a reward, and a number of slots.
- Users complete the required on-chain action to participate and earn the reward.
- Rewards settle in USDC on Arc.
- Campaigns end automatically when their slots fill, so they're self-limiting.

## Why they matter

A new project's biggest problem is the cold start: no users means no activity, no activity means no trust. Campaigns give founders a way to put real usage on the board — which in turn feeds the [trust layer](/trust-layer) (the `Established` modifier reflects sustained, real activity) and makes a project a stronger candidate for [Lens AI](/lens-ai) payouts.

## Running one

Create and manage campaigns from your project dashboard. You control the reward, the slots, and the action users must take.
