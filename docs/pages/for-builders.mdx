# For builders

How to go from unknown to discoverable, cited, and **paid** by Lens AI.

<Steps>

### List your project

Add it on the [Ecosystem](/ecosystem). It gets indexed, its metrics are tracked, and it enters the [trust layer](/trust-layer) at the `Listed` tier.

### Claim & verify

Claim ownership with your wallet to reach `Claimed`, then verify to reach `Verified`. Higher trust means higher payout weight and better visibility.

### Connect a wallet

Connect the wallet that should receive payouts via your [Builder Profile](/builder-profiles). Until you do, earnings **accrue and are held** until you claim them — nothing is sent to an unproven address.

### Earn automatically

When Lens AI's answers rely on your data, you earn — a fraction of a cent in USDC, on-chain, every time. Run a [campaign](/campaigns) to put real usage on the board and pick up the `Established` modifier.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Track your standing and earnings any time on the [Lens AI page](https://arclenz.xyz/lens) and your project dashboard.
</Callout>

## What you can't game

You can't pay your way to a payout. Lens AI only pays Verified / Established / Claimed **Arc-native** builders, with per-day caps and per-caller de-duplication. The way to earn more is simple: build something real that the ecosystem's AI actually relies on.
