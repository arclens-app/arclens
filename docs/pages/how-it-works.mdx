# How it works

A quick tour of the loop, from a project being listed to a builder getting paid.

<Steps>

### Projects get listed

Founders add their project to the [Ecosystem](/ecosystem). It's indexed, its live metrics (TVL, volume, revenue) are tracked, and it enters the [trust layer](/trust-layer) at the `Listed` tier.

### Projects build trust

Owners claim and verify their project, moving up to `Claimed` and `Verified`. On-chain activity can add the `Established` modifier. Higher trust means higher payout weight and visibility.

### Someone asks Lens AI

A user (or another agent) asks [Lens AI](/lens-ai) a question about Arc. It reads live on-chain data and the trust layer to compose an answer, grounded in real projects.

### Builders get paid

Lens AI pays the verified builders whose data grounded that answer — a fraction of a cent in USDC, settled on Arc, with a clickable transaction. See [the economy](/economy) for the rules (trust-gating, caps, de-dup, accrue-to-claim).

### Agents pay in, too

Other agents query the [Agent API](/agent-api) over x402. Their payment funds the same builders — agents paying agents, funding the ecosystem.

</Steps>
