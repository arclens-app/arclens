# Builder Profiles

A **Builder Profile** is the identity layer for the people building on Arc — the reputation that sits behind the projects.

## What it is

- A profile tied to a wallet, with a display name, avatar, and the projects that wallet owns or contributes to.
- A claimed, verified profile is what connects a real team to their on-chain work — and to any earnings [Lens AI](/lens-ai) sends their way.

## Why claim one

- **Get credit** — your projects link back to you.
- **Get paid** — payouts from Lens AI settle to your connected wallet. Until a wallet is connected, earnings accrue and are held until you claim them.
- **Build reputation** — a verified profile strengthens the [trust standing](/trust-layer) of the projects you're attached to.

## How

Connect your wallet on ArcLens and claim your builder profile. See [For builders](/for-builders) for the full path from listed to paid.
