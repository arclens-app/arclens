# Explorer

The **Explorer** is one surface of ArcLens — useful, but not the headline. It lets anyone inspect activity on Arc and verify what the rest of the platform claims.

## What you can look up

- **Transactions** — status, value, gas, decoded input, and token transfers. A USDC transfer is detected and surfaced with a value-flow view.
- **Addresses** — balances and activity for any wallet or contract.
- **Live network** — current block and gas.

## Arc specifics

- **USDC is the gas token** on Arc, so fees are denominated in USDC.
- Sub-second finality.
- Open any transaction directly: `arclenz.xyz/tx/<hash>`.

## Why it matters here

Every [Lens AI](/lens-ai) payout links to its on-chain transaction on this explorer — so a payout receipt is one click from being independently verified, without ever leaving ArcLens.
