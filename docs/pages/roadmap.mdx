# Roadmap

ArcLens started as a hub for Arc. The direction is to make it **agent-powered** end to end, with Lens AI at the center.

## Now

- Ecosystem directory, campaigns, trust layer, and explorer — live.
- Lens AI answering questions and **paying verified builders** on-chain.
- The [Agent API](/agent-api) — a pay-per-call trust oracle other agents can query.

## Next

- **Richer agent actions** — deeper metrics, comparisons, and ecosystem queries over the API.
- **Production x402** — moving from the testnet `sim` proof to full on-chain payment verification.
- **Agent-run surfaces** — Lens AI helping run campaigns and surface trust, not just answer.

## Later

- ArcLens as an **agent-powered platform** — the intelligence layer for Arc, where the mascot and agent power the experience rather than sitting beside it.

<Callout type="info">
This roadmap is directional, not a commitment to dates. We build in the open — follow [@arclens_app](https://x.com/arclens_app) for releases.
</Callout>
