# Lens AI

**Lens AI is the agent at the center of ArcLens** — and our mascot. It used to be a chatbox you could ask about projects. Now it has a wallet, and it pays the builders it learns from.

## What it does

Ask it anything about Arc. It reads **live on-chain data** and the **[trust layer](/trust-layer)** to compose an answer, grounded in real projects. Then it does the part nobody else does: it **pays the verified builders whose work grounded that answer** — a fraction of a cent in USDC, settled on Arc, with a clickable receipt.

<Callout type="info">
The payment isn't income — at these amounts it's a **signal**. The ecosystem's own AI, publicly and on-chain, vouching for the teams that proved useful.
</Callout>

## How payouts stay honest

Every payout is:

- **Trust-gated** — only Verified, Established, or Claimed Arc-native builders. Never infrastructure, the chain, or risk-flagged projects.
- **Capped** — per answer and per day.
- **De-duplicated** — the same asker can't repeatedly fund the same builder within a 24h window.
- **Accrue-to-claim** — if a trusted builder hasn't connected a wallet yet, earnings accrue and are held until they claim them.

See [The nanopayment economy](/economy) for the full model, and the [Agent API](/agent-api) to query Lens AI from your own agent.
